Travel Tips

Best Time to Visit

May to August is ideal for sightseeing and events.

Use Public Transport

Use MRT, LRT, Monorail, or e-hailing services to move around easily.

Dress Modestly

When visiting religious sites, dress appropriately.

Currency

Malaysian Ringgit (MYR) is the official currency.

Safety Tips

Keep your belongings safe and stay aware of your surroundings.
