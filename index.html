About Kuala Lumpur

Kuala Lumpur (KL) is Malaysia's vibrant capital, a multicultural hub known for its blend of modern skyscrapers like the iconic Petronas Twin Towers and historic Art Deco buildings, rich cultural melting pot (Malay, Chinese, Indian), and diverse food scene, all situated at the confluence of the Klang and Gombak rivers, earning its name "muddy confluence". It offers a mix of bustling city life, serene green spaces like KLCC Park, extensive shopping, and significant cultural sites such as Batu Caves and various temples and mosques.
